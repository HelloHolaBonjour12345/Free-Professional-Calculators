Consistent saving is one of the most reliable strategies for long-term financial stability. By combining regular contributions with compound interest, individuals can steadily build wealth over time.

This savings calculator estimates the future value of your savings based on an initial deposit, monthly contributions, annual interest rate and investment duration.

The calculation assumes monthly compounding of interest, which reflects most savings accounts and investment vehicles. Even small monthly deposits can grow significantly when compounded consistently.

Understanding projected savings growth supports better financial planning, retirement preparation and long-term goal setting.
